Resetting my quantitative trading project

June 25, 2026 · 3 min read

I recently deleted a bunch of my old repositories (like GeminiQuant and MEXC-HFT). This wasn't an accident. It was a conscious reset.

Why reset?

Over time, the codebase for those projects became highly fragmented. I had too many disconnected ideas and not enough rigorous testing. Quantitative trading requires a solid mathematical and systematic foundation, not just cobbling APIs together.

What's next?

I am now consolidating my efforts into AgentQuantV2. The focus will be on AI-assisted research, cleaner architecture, and proper backtesting before any live execution. It's better to have one reliable system than five broken ones.
