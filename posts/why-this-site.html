Why I’m building this public lab

Published on 07 / 08 / 2026

Welcome to my public lab. As a dental student, I find myself exploring many fields outside of dentistry—from artificial intelligence and coding, to quantitative finance and systems thinking.

I realized I needed a place to consolidate these thoughts. Not a polished "influencer" blog, but a working laboratory. A place where I can store notes, track my experimental projects, and reflect on what I've learned.

The goal

The goal is simply documentation and clarity. By writing things down publicly, I force myself to organize my thoughts more logically. It helps me step back and see the bigger picture, whether I am revising for my dental OSCEs or writing a new trading algorithm.

Why keep it simple?

I chose to build this site with pure HTML and CSS. No massive frameworks, no complex databases. Just text files stored on GitHub. This aligns with my philosophy: focus on the content and the system, not the overhead.
