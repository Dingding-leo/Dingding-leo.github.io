Scope small, track ruthlessly, and ship it. Building solo requires strict discipline.

## Rule 1: Scope it down
If you think it will take 2 weeks, it will take 2 months. Cut the features in half before you write a single line of code.

## Rule 2: Iterate
Version 1 is supposed to be embarrassing. If it's not, you launched too late.
